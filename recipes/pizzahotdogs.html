Pizza Hot Dogs

Description

Pizza Hot Dogs are a combination of hot dogs on a bun that are topped with cheese, pepperoni, and tomato sauce

The recipe was made famous by the shop Orange Julius. Orange Julius used to be a mall store that served smoothies and different hot dogs such as the Chicago Dog, the Miami Dog, and the Pizza Dog. It faded into obscurity in the middle of the 2000's and returned to making smoothies.

Ingredients

  • 4 hot dogs
  • 4 buns
  • 24 pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of tomato or pizza sauce
  • 1/3 cup of shedded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Boil hot dogs in water until cooked
  2. Put 6 pepperonis on each bun and top with the boiled hot dog
  3. Spoon 1 tablespoon of pizza sauce onto each hot dog bun and top with mozzarella
  4. Put hot dogs on baking sheet and bake for 2-3 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly (alternatively, put in microwave for 30 seconds)
  5. Pull out of oven or microwave and serve on a plate
